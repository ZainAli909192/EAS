Nurturing Young Minds with Loving Care


Welcome to Blooming TOTS Dayhome in Canada, a safe, nurturing space led by Salma Razaq . With love, patience, physical engaging activities, we foster your child’s growth, learning, and creativity in a home-like environment filled with joy.
Our mission is to nurture children's growth through creative play, hands-on learning, and loving guidance. Salma’s passion for child development ensures every child feels valued, safe, and inspired to explore, learn, and thrive every day.
